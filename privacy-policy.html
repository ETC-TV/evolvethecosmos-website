Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Evolve The Cosmos LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit evolvethecosmos.com (the "Site"), purchase our digital products or services, or interact with our content on third-party platforms including YouTube and TikTok.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Site. Questions or concerns? Contact us at support@evolvethecosmos.com.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy applies to all information collected through our Site, digital products, email communications, and any related services.

// 02

Information We Collect Directly

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you purchase a product, subscribe to our email list, or contact us. The categories of information we may collect include:

Identity & Contact Data

  • Names — collected at email signup and checkout
  • Email addresses — core to our ConvertKit email marketing and order processing
  • Contact preferences — email marketing opt-in and opt-out preferences

Financial Data

  • Billing addresses — collected at checkout for digital product purchases
  • Payment card information — processed securely through Stripe and Gumroad. We do not store raw card numbers on our systems.

Sensitive Information

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers, government identification numbers, health or medical data, biometric identifiers, racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, sexual orientation, or precise geolocation data.

Minors

We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age. If we learn that personal information from users under 18 has been collected, we will deactivate the account and delete such data promptly.

// 03

Automatically Collected Data

We do not operate tracking scripts, cookies, pixels, beacons, or behavioral analytics tools on our Site. We do not collect derivative data such as IP addresses, device fingerprints, browser types, or individual user geolocation data.

Third-party platforms we publish content on — including YouTube (Google LLC) and TikTok — collect their own analytics data from viewers independently, under their own privacy policies. We receive only aggregated, anonymized performance metrics (such as total views or engagement rates) through their APIs, not individual user data.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other third-party tracking technology on our owned web properties.

// 04

Google API Services

We use the YouTube Data API v3 as a core component of our automated content publishing pipeline. This integration allows us to upload videos, manage metadata, organize playlists, and retrieve aggregate performance analytics.

Our use of Google API Services is subject to the Google Privacy Policy. We do not use Google API data to develop, improve, or train generalized AI or machine learning models. We do not sell Google API data or use it for serving advertisements.

We do not use Google Maps API, Google Analytics API, Google Drive API, or Gmail API in our operations.

Artificial Intelligence Services

Evolve The Cosmos operates an AI-powered content automation platform. Our AI infrastructure is built on a local-first architecture, meaning the majority of AI processing occurs on our own hardware rather than third-party cloud services.

Local AI Infrastructure

We operate self-hosted AI models including DeepSeek and Qwen language models via Ollama, and image generation via ComfyUI. Data processed through these systems remains on our local infrastructure and is not transmitted to external AI providers.

Third-Party AI Services

We use ElevenLabs for AI voice synthesis. Script text is transmitted to ElevenLabs for audio generation. No personal user data is included in these transmissions.

Your Right to Opt Out

You have the right to opt out of having your personal information processed by our AI-based services. To exercise this right, contact us at support@evolvethecosmos.com with your request. We will process your opt-out within 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you maintain an active relationship with us — meaning an active email subscription, an active digital product license, or an ongoing service engagement.

Purchase records and billing information are retained for a minimum of seven (7) years in accordance with IRS financial record-keeping requirements, regardless of your subscription status.

When you request deletion of your data, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it or where retention is necessary to resolve disputes or enforce our agreements.

Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Local-First Architecture: User data processed through our pipeline is handled on our private local cluster infrastructure rather than public cloud services
  • Encrypted Network Tunnels: All inter-system communication runs through Tailscale encrypted VPN connections
  • Containerized Services: All services run in isolated Docker containers limiting exposure between systems
  • Credential Management: Active security hygiene including regular API key rotation and access control reviews
  • No Public LLM Exposure: Sensitive data is never transmitted to public AI inference endpoints

Despite our safeguards, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We cannot promise or guarantee that hackers, cybercriminals, or other unauthorized third parties will not be able to defeat our security and improperly collect, access, steal, or modify your information.

EU & UK Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR respectively.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to fulfill your digital product purchase or service agreement
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for our business operations where not overridden by your rights
  • Consent: Processing based on your explicit consent, such as email marketing subscriptions
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required to comply with applicable law

Your GDPR Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your personal data, and to object to its processing. You also have the right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may not provide the same level of data protection as your home country. We implement appropriate safeguards for such transfers in accordance with applicable law.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. EU residents may also use the European Commission's Online Dispute Resolution platform at ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr.
